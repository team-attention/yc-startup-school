# YC Startup School — Module 6: Growing and Monetizing

Module 6 covers the frameworks for measuring and accelerating growth after you have initial customers. YC partners Tom Blomfield, Divya Bhat, Aaron Epstein, and David Lieb share how to set KPIs, understand your business model, price for B2B, improve retention, and use AI coding tools effectively.

## Key Topics

**Lecture 1 — Setting KPIs and Goals (Divya Bhat)**
- How to choose the right primary metric (the one metric that matters)
- OKR-style goal setting for early-stage startups
- Weekly growth rate targets (5–7% week-over-week as a benchmark)
- Leading vs. lagging indicators

**Lecture 2 — Key Startup Metrics (Tom Blomfield)**
- Revenue metrics: MRR, ARR, ACV
- Growth metrics: WoW growth, MoM growth
- Engagement metrics by business type
- Churn vs. retention and how to think about both
- How YC evaluates metrics during application review

**Lecture 3 — YC Guide to Business Models (Aaron Epstein)**
- The full spectrum of business models: SaaS, marketplace, transactional, advertising, hardware, usage-based
- Unit economics: LTV, CAC, payback period
- Which business models YC has the most success with
- Revenue quality: recurring vs. one-time

**Lecture 4 — Consumer Startup Metrics (Tom Blomfield)**
- DAU/MAU ratio as engagement signal
- Retention curves: what good looks like for consumer apps
- D1, D7, D30 retention benchmarks by category
- How to diagnose why users churn

**Lecture 5 — How to Improve Cohort Retention (David Lieb)**
- Cohort analysis fundamentals
- Identifying the "aha moment" and time-to-value
- Retention levers: onboarding, activation, habit formation
- Qualitative research methods to understand churn

**Lecture 6 — How to Price for B2B (Tom Blomfield)**
- Value-based pricing vs. cost-plus vs. competitive pricing
- Common B2B pricing mistakes (starting too low, not raising prices)
- Pricing tiers and packaging
- How to have the pricing conversation with prospects

**Lecture 7 — How to Get the Most Out of Vibe Coding (Tom Blomfield)**
- Using AI coding assistants to build faster
- Prompting strategies for code generation
- When vibe coding works vs. when it breaks down
- Maintaining code quality with AI-assisted development

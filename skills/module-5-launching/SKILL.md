# YC Startup School — Module 5: Launching

Module 5 covers the critical early-stage challenge of launching and getting your first customers. YC partners Kat Mañalac, Gustaf Alströmer, and Aaron Epstein share tactical frameworks for launching repeatedly, acquiring early users manually, and writing cold emails that convert.

## Key Topics

**Lecture 1 — The Best Way to Launch Your Startup (Kat Mañalac)**
- Why startups should launch multiple times, not just once
- Types of launches: Hunt launches, community launches, Press/media, social posts, Friends/family/colleagues
- Crafting a one-sentence pitch that resonates
- YC Launch posts on HN (Show HN strategy)
- When to launch: launch early, launch often

**Lecture 2 — How to Get Your First Customers (Gustaf Alströmer)**
- "Do things that don't scale" — the Paul Graham doctrine
- Manual vs. automated customer acquisition in early stage
- Where to find your first users: direct outreach, communities, personal networks
- How Airbnb got early users (Gustaf's own experience)
- Converting early users into evangelists

**Lecture 3 — How To Convert Customers With Cold Emails (Aaron Epstein)**
- Mapping your cold outreach funnel (delivery → open → reply → meeting → close)
- Finding email addresses effectively
- Writing subject lines that get opened
- Email body structure: short, personal, clear CTA
- Follow-up sequences and timing
- Measuring and iterating on cold email performance

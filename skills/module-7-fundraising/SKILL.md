## Key Topics

**Lecture 1 — How Startup Fundraising Works (Brad Flora)**
- The typical fundraising journey: bootstrapping → pre-seed → seed → Series A
- SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity) mechanics: valuation cap, discount, MFN
- Priced rounds vs. convertible instruments
- How to approach investors: warm intro vs. cold outreach
- Investor psychology: what makes them say yes
- The fundraising timeline and process management
- Common fundraising mistakes founders make

**Lecture 2 — How to Apply and Succeed at YC (Dalton Caldwell)**
- What YC actually looks for in founders and companies
- The YC application: what questions matter most and why
- Common mistakes in YC applications
- The YC interview: what to expect, how to prepare
- What happens during a YC batch
- YC's network and long-term value beyond funding
- Advice on when to apply (early and often)

**Lecture 3 — Starting a Company: The Key Terms You Should Know (Dalton Caldwell)**
- Equity fundamentals: cap table, shares, dilution
- SAFE terms explained: post-money SAFE, pro-rata rights
- Common stock vs. preferred stock
- Option pool and employee equity
- Term sheet components: valuation, board seats, liquidation preference
- Key documents: incorporation, IP assignment, NDAs
- How to choose lawyers and avoid common legal pitfalls
